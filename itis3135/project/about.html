What This is About:

This is a club stationed at Western Carolina University with a focus on going outside and hiking with the surrounding mountains.

Whether you are experienced or a beginner to hiking we welcome you to join us and have fun.

Don't know if you want to join yet? Come join us for a few events and become apart of the community before commiting to our club!

This is an ever growing community and we hope to see new members with excitement to join us.

Notes:

  • We as a club hold fundraisers to gain funds for our events but feel free to fund your own events.
  • Events will be updated as soon as organizers are ready and updates will be emailed if necessary
  • Events can be organized by anyone and scheduled whenever. However, weekends are most preferred times.
