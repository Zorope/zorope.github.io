What This is About:

This is a club stationed at Western Carolina University with a focus on going outside and hiking with the surrounding mountains. Whether you are experienced or a beginner to hiking we welcome you to join us and have fun. Don't know if you want to join yet? Come join us for a few events and become apart of the community before commiting to our club! This is an ever growing community and we hope to see new members with excitement to join us.

Why Join Our Club?:

Our club is perfect for you if you are looking for a community centered club that values relationships and experience. We are constantly looking to improve our community and events so we are very open to opinions from everyone and will work with feedback. Although we have leadership roles its not necessary to have direct impact on club choices and you can always contact leaders for special requests. You don't have to commit to any events and we always welcome anyone to join. Joining the club is optional but joining our community can benefit us and you since we will be working towards a common goal.

Leadership:

The club as of right now does not have a voting system for a new club leader. This club is made purely for fun and the joy of hiking. We try our best to support the community and include everyone so the leader role of mainly to give a sense of leadership. However, we are all working towards a common goal of creating a great community. We do have other roles to maintain order and records of club expenses and activities. Feel free to contact our club leader to ask about other club roles if you are interested.

Notes:

We as a club hold fundraisers to gain funds for our events but feel free to fund your own events.

Events will be updated as soon as organizers are ready and updates will be emailed if necessary.

Events can be organized by anyone and scheduled whenever. However, weekends are most preferred times.
