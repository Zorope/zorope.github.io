Website Evaluations


Good: Nvidia

Site to promote their products, upcoming events, and community related programs

  • C: Text and images clearly pop from the background. Nothing blends into each other and clearly displays information.
  • R: Has good repettion with labeled sections and four articles under it. Clean spacing between words and sections.
  • A: Not a lot of text but articles are lined up. Text within article preview boxes are left aligned showing consistency.
  • P: Clear section headers with related items under it. Consistent space between sections and items under section.

Bad: Stanly Early College Highschool

Site to post about their school events, school registration, and college related news

  • C: Images aren't very high quality so when text overlap the images it looks bad. Certain colors and images don't pop enough.
  • R: Text size and image sizes differ drastically across the page. Also navigation boxes have differing design and sizes.
  • A: Text and images are mainly centered aligned. Leaving an overall good alignment for the page.
  • P: Page has section headers and has related info below it. Related images are close and gives good proximity for the page.
